European Adventure

The sky glows orange as the sun creeps behind the London skyline. You and your buddies sit on the balcony of your apartment sipping on Red Stripes and Estrella’s. It’s your semester abroad and Europe has endless countries to travel to while you are there. Spring break is around the corner and you guys need to figure out a destination to go to. Tom asks you as he sips his drink, “So where should we go? I want to be on the beach somewhere.” You stare at the night sky and ponder which coastal European countries would be the best to explore. You narrow your options to Venice, Italy and Alicante, Spain.

If you want to go to Venice, Enter Italy in the box. If you want to go to Alicante, Enter Spain in the box. (Actually click the button. Do not press enter.)
