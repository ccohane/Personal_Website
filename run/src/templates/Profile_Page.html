Callahan Cohane's Bio

Callahan Cohane is a senior business administration major with a concentration in finance at Ithaca College and looks forward to graduating in May 2018. Throughout college, Callahan has been working towards a career in the financial services industry and specifically is passionate about equities. Callahan has participated as a Junior Analyst for the Core Trading Consultants at Ithaca College where he works in various sector groups to analyze publically traded companies that should be bought, sold, or trimmed from the Investment Club’s portfolio. Previously, Callahan worked as a Finance Intern for Lionshare Farm in Greenwich, CT where he acted as an accounts payable clerk, reconciled company books and created expense analyses for management to accurately forecast future expenses. Callahan has also developed his computer science skills by being a Certified Microsoft Excel Specialist and is familiar with many programming languages including Java, Python, Java Script, HTML, and CSS. Callahan also enjoys participating in the Men’s Ultimate Frisbee Club as well as many other intramural sports.
